Outreach:

  • FLL Robotics

    Overview:

    Get community service hours by mentoring elementary school students as they make their own robot using EV3. The schools we volunteer at are Los Alamitos Elementary and Almaden Elementary. These teams participate in FLL competitions every year. Meetings at Almaden are every Wednesday and Thursday from 3:15 to 4:15, while Los Alamitos is on Wednesdays from 3:15 to 4:30. Please check the events tab for more information.

  • VEX Robotics

    Overview:

    Participate in meetings at Castillero Middle school to work with middle school students and get community service hours. This category has 2 sub teams and they are divided based on prior knowledge on building tactics. The team will adopt a new platform for the advanced sub team called VEX EDR, which is the more advanced version of the VEX platform that we are using right now. Both sub teams participate in FTC competitions. The meetings are at Castillero Middle School from 4:00 to 5:00 every week Monday through Friday. Please check the events tab for more information.
