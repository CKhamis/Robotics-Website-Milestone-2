  • Overview:

    The media team shoots photos and records games as well as edit video to view online (Youtube). This is a category that can be joined along with an additional category because it is not a very time demanding course. If you are in this category, please bring your own camera. When you join this team, you will have priority over everybody else on the team when attending our games, post to put social media pages, and will have the ability to edit and add to our media database. We use a number of programs for editing photos and video like Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, and Premiere pro.

    The current head of the Media division is Riley Stokes.
